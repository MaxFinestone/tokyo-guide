About This Guide

A love letter to the world's most extraordinary city

Tokyo Tourist is a curated travel guide written in October 2023 by Maxence Finestone after a trip that confirmed a long-held suspicion: nowhere else on earth combines ancient tradition and radical modernity as beautifully as this city does.

This guide covers the attractions, neighbourhoods, restaurants, and cultural customs that every visitor to Tokyo should know. It aims to be genuinely useful: curated with real opinions, practical details, and enough context to help you feel oriented from the moment you land.

Every article was written after personally visiting the location, eating the food, taking the trains, and getting gloriously, productively lost. The recommendations are mine alone, and I stand behind all of them.

What's Covered

Four main sections: Attractions (temples, towers, parks, museums), Neighborhoods (Shibuya, Ginza, Asakusa, Ueno), Eat & Drink (Michelin counters to cat cafés), Culture & Etiquette (the customs that make your trip smoother), and a Seasonal guide to cherry blossom season.
